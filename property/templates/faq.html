Frequently Asked Questions

How can we help you?

Working with Parcel

Parcel helps you to reduce your property bill. The property tax code is as complex as the income tax code, where tools like TurboTax make it easy for you. However, no such tools exist for property taxes. We ask you a few questions, and analyze your local property tax deductions based on your county's tax code. We then help you file with the county for various deductions.

By law in the United States, property taxes are public record. This is why, for instance, you can see the property tax amount when looking at any property on Zillow.com.

Security is core to what we do. Whenever we ask for personal information when filing deductions with the county, your information is securely encrypted and only processed by computers. Our program is designed to meet or exceed industry standards with many different controls to keep your personal information safe.

Billing & Services

We provide notifications regarding late or non-payment free of charge. This allows you to avoid any late payments on your property tax bill.

For our paid subscription, we charge an annual fee up front.

We will compile and send deduction paperwork to your local county office with a paid subscription. We will also continue to monitor the local tax code to ensure that if any changes arise, you will be able to file for additional deductions.
